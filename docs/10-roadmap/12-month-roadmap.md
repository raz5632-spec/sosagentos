# Twelve-Month Roadmap (Aug 2026 – Jun 2027)

| Period | Phase | Workstreams |
|---|---|---|
| Aug 2026 | Foundation | Repo, CI, monorepo, CLAUDE package; core schema, IAM, tenancy |
| Sep–Oct 2026 | Core Intelligence | ACP, agent runtime, model router; knowledge ingestion + retrieval; governance, audit, rollback |
| Oct–Dec 2026 | Product Modules | Education + Student OS; content engine + DNA engine; analytics + CEO dashboard |
| Nov 2026–Feb 2027 | Integrations | Google connectors; Meta WhatsApp + Instagram; Canva integration |
| Jan–Mar 2027 | Advanced Capabilities | Competitor intelligence; automation workflows on Temporal; Digital Twin simulations |
| Mar–May 2027 | Hardening & Launch | Staging hardening, monitoring, backups; App Review + external readiness; UAT and rollout |
| May 2027 | Phase 1 GA | Internal S.O.S. production |
| Jun 2027 | Post-launch | Optimization, Phase 2 multi-tenant productization prep |
